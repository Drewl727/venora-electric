What We Do

Complete Electrical Services

From routine repairs to full panel upgrades, we handle every aspect of residential and commercial electrical work with the same attention to quality we’ve maintained since 1960.

Residential

Wiring, outlets, fixtures, and complete home electrical systems. We treat your home with the same care we’d give our own.

Commercial

Service upgrades, new construction wiring, lighting retrofits, and code compliance work for businesses across Hartford County.

Service & Repair

Fast, reliable repairs for any electrical problem. Most issues diagnosed and resolved in a single visit.
