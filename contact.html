Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Do you provide free estimates?

Yes. We provide written estimates before any work begins. Call or fill out the form above to schedule an on-site evaluation.

How quickly can you respond to an emergency?

We answer emergency calls 24/7. In most cases, we can have a licensed electrician on-site within hours of your call.

Do you handle permit and inspection requirements?

Yes. We obtain all required permits and arrange final inspections for qualifying projects. It’s included in the scope of work.

What towns do you serve?

Bloomfield, West Hartford, Hartford, East Hartford, Avon, Windsor, Simsbury, Windsor Locks, East Windsor, Granby, and surrounding towns. Call if you’re unsure.
