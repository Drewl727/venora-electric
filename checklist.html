Free Self-Assessment

Home Electrical Safety Checklist

Ten yes/no questions. Two minutes. Find out whether your home’s electrical system needs attention — before a problem finds you.

How It Works

Each “Yes” answer represents a known electrical risk factor. Answer all 10 and we’ll score your home and tell you what to do next.

0 – 2 Yes

Your home looks good. A routine inspection is still a smart move every few years.

3 – 5 Yes

Some items need attention. Schedule a safety inspection soon.

6 – 10 Yes

Potential safety concerns. Call us today — don’t wait.

Is Your Home’s Electrical System Safe?

Answer honestly. “Yes” answers indicate potential risk factors.

0 of 10 answered
1 My home was built before 1980.
2 I have a fuse box, not a circuit breaker panel.
3 I use extension cords as permanent wiring in some rooms.
4 Outlets near my sinks or bathrooms do not have GFCI protection.
5 My circuits trip or blow fuses more than once a month.
6 I have two-prong (ungrounded) outlets in my home.
7 My lights flicker or dim regularly.
8 I’ve noticed outlets or switches that feel warm to the touch.
9 I don’t have interconnected smoke detectors on every floor.
10 My home has never had a professional electrical inspection.
