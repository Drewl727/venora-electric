Our Story

About Venora Electric

A family business built on reputation, referrals, and the belief that doing the job right the first time is the only way to do it.

Founded 1960

Sixty-Five Years in Your Neighborhood

Venora Electric was founded in 1960 in Bloomfield, Connecticut — the same year Kennedy took office, and the same year many of the homes we still service today were first wired. What started as a one-man operation built on honest work and fair prices has grown into a multi-generational family business trusted by thousands of households across Greater Hartford County.

Tim Venora leads the company today, carrying forward the same values instilled by the family’s founders: show up on time, explain what you’re doing, charge a fair price, and clean up when you leave. It’s not complicated — but it’s exactly what customers tell us makes us different.

Our reputation has been built almost entirely on referrals. We’ve rewired homes for the children and grandchildren of our original customers. We’ve serviced the same commercial buildings for decades. That kind of loyalty isn’t bought — it’s earned, call by call and job by job.

We are fully licensed under Connecticut’s Electrical Unlimited Contractor License and carry complete general liability and workers’ compensation insurance. Every job is permitted and inspected when required. We don’t cut corners — not because we have to, but because that’s how we were raised to do business.

Credentials & Licensing

Licensed, Insured & Code-Compliant

Every job we do is performed by licensed electricians under a valid Connecticut Unlimited Electrical Contractor license. We carry full general liability and workers’ compensation insurance on every project.

CT Electrical Unlimited Contractor License

License No. ELC.0125773-E1 — Issued by the Connecticut Department of Consumer Protection. Valid for all residential, commercial, and industrial electrical work in the State of Connecticut.

Fully Insured

General liability insurance and workers’ compensation coverage on every project. Certificates of insurance available upon request for commercial clients and property managers.

Code & Permit Compliance

All work performed to current National Electrical Code (NEC) standards and Connecticut state amendments. We obtain required permits and arrange inspections for all qualifying projects.
